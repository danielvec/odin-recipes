My favorite cookies

Over the last few months I've been trying to eat healthier. As a cookie-addict, I needed to find a healthier alternative to my favorite snack, so I came up with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup Almond Butter
  • 1/4 cup Lankato Monkfruit Sweetener
  • 1/2 cup Rolled Oats

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Mix together ingredients
  3. Place balls of cookie dough onto lightly greased baking sheet
  4. Bake for 8 minutes
