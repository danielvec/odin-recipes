Almond butter waffles

I love almond butter. I also love waffles. So I just had to make a waggle with almond butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup Almond Butter
  • 1/4 cup Protein Powder
  • 1/2 cup Rolled Oats

Directions

  1. Preheat waffle iron
  2. Blend together ingredients
  3. Fill iron with mixed ingredients
  4. Cook for 5 minutes
