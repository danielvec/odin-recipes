Peanut Butter Cups

Tasty Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups

Here's the recipe for my wife's favorite treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 dark chocolate bar
  • 1/4 cup Peanut Butter
  • cupcake liners

Directions

  1. Heat chocolate until melted
  2. Fill cupcake liners halfway with melted chocolate and cool in freezer
  3. Fill with peanut butter and put more chocolate on top
  4. Cool in freezer until hardened
